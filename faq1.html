How do I place an order?

Visit the website at www.baazs.com and browse the available products.
Select the items you want to purchase by clicking the "Add to Cart" button.
Proceed to checkout by clicking the "Checkout" button in the cart section.
Fill in the required information, including your name, email, and shipping address.
Choose your preferred payment method and enter your payment details.
Review your order and submit it by clicking the "Place Order" button.
