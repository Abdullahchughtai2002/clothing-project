Formation

Baazs is a new clothing company that was founded with the aim of
providing high-quality and trendy clothing to its customers.

create a brand that would stand out in the market. Baazs has a unique
design philosophy that is centered on creating sustainable and ethical clothing.

and aims to provide a personalized shopping experience to its customers.
